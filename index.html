About TT

I'm Theresa Tichich. I live in St. Paul near Como Lake. I worked at the University of Minnesota for many years in information technology. Four years ago I was able to retire early and now teach part-time and do freelance work. My last position at the University of Minnesota was at the College of Design as their web manager and developer. Over the course of my career at the College of Design, I managed about 20 sites. Many of those site's designs changed 3-4 years. Here's a screenshot of the last design I worked on:

Camping is a great joy for me! I love nature! Hiking and kayaking and sitting by the campfire. Nature provides great material for photography, which is also one of my hobbies.

Oh, and my two favorite foods are, undoubtedly, chocolate and ice cream!

On my Nintendo Switch, I play Animal Crossing almost every day. On my Wii, I play Just Dance and the original NES 8-bit hockey. Super fun!
